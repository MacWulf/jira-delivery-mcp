# Derived Acceptance

Do not ask a separate acceptance and validation block by default.

## Derive from prior answers

Use:
- target state
- current pain
- stakeholder expectations
- scope boundaries
- constraints

## Produce

- `derived_acceptance_direction`
- `candidate_acceptance_criteria`
- `validation_hints`

## Derivation prompts

Ask yourself:
- what change would count as success
- who would judge it as good enough
- what evidence would likely prove that
- what failure mode must disappear

## Example shape

- expected business outcome
- likely validating role
- candidate evidence type
- first-pass acceptance criteria bullets
